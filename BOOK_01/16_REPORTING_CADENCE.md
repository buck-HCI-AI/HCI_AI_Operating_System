## Reporting Philosophy

Reports should require zero assembly time. If someone is manually compiling a report from email, spreadsheets, or memory, the system is failing that person.

HCI AI produces all reports from the project record. The human role in reporting is review, context, and sign-off — not compilation.

## What Is In Each Report

### Morning Brief (auto-delivered 7 AM)
- Today's weather (temp, conditions, precipitation chance)
- Top 3 schedule priorities from look-ahead
- Open flags from prior day
- Budget alerts active
- Approvals or decisions waiting for Buck
- RFIs and submittals overdue

### Weekly Status Report (client-facing)
- Period: dates covered
- Contract summary: original + approved changes = current contract value
- Percent complete (schedule and cost basis)
- Work completed this period: by scope/area
- Work planned next period
- Open items: RFIs, submittals, change orders
- Issues and risks
- Photos: 2-5 representative shots of progress
- Buck sign-off line

### Executive Summary (Buck internal)
- Company-level KPIs vs. prior week
- Active project traffic lights (one row per project)
- Alert feed: what needs Buck's attention
- Bid pipeline: proposals and opportunities
- Decision queue: what is awaiting Buck's approval
- Win/loss since last report
