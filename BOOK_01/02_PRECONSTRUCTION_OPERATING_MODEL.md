## The Preconstruction Chain

Preconstruction is not a set of independent tasks — it is a chain. Each link depends on the previous one. A broken link produces a bad bid, a bad buyout, or a bad project.

The chain:

```
SOP 01 Opportunity Intake
  → SOP 02 Project Intake
    → SOP 03 File Organization
      → SOP 04 Plan Review
        → SOP 05 Construction Narrative
          → SOP 06 Missing Information / Risk Log
            → SOP 07 ROM Budget
              → SOP 08 Historical Cost Database
                → SOP 09 Budget Review
                  → SOP 10 Allowances / Alternates / Exclusions
                    → SOP 11 Bid Package ← PILOT CONVERSION COMPLETE
                      → SOP 12 Subcontractor CRM
                        → SOP 13 Bid Distribution
                          → SOP 14 Bid Follow-Up
                            → SOP 15 Bid Leveling ← PILOT CONVERSION COMPLETE
                              → SOP 16 Buyout
                                → (Field Execution Phase)
```

---

## Why the Chain Matters

**If plan review (SOP 04) is incomplete:** The scope in SOP 11 will have gaps. Subs will bid on different things. The leveling sheet will show an apples-to-oranges comparison. The low bid will be the one that excluded the most.

**If the bid package (SOP 11) is poorly assembled:** Addenda will multiply. Scope clarification calls will consume estimating time. The leveling process becomes interpretation instead of comparison.

**If leveling (SOP 15) is skipped or rushed:** Award decisions are made on incomplete information. Post-award scope gaps become change orders. Margin erodes.

**If buyout (SOP 16) is not documented:** Subcontract agreements miss scope items. Disputes arise over what was included.

## Owner (Buck) Approval Points in Preconstruction

Buck must approve before work advances past these gates:

| Gate | What Requires Approval | Document |
|------|------------------------|---------|
| ROM Budget complete | Budget released for distribution | SOP 09 deliverable |
| Bid Package issued | Scope package sent to subs | SOP 11 Approved status |
| Award decision | Subcontract awarded to specific sub | SOP 15 / SOP 16 — Buck signs |
| Buyout complete | All major trades under contract | SOP 16 closeout record |

AI may prepare every input to these decisions. AI may not make the decision.

---

## AI Role in Preconstruction

| Task | AI Does | Human Approves |
|------|---------|---------------|
| Plan review | Reads drawings, flags missing info, generates scope checklist | PM reviews; Buck confirms risk log |
| Budget | Pulls historical costs, checks against ROM, flags variances | Estimator reviews; Buck approves release |
| Bid package | Drafts scope sections from drawings and narrative | PM reviews completeness; Buck approves issue |
| Sub outreach | Pulls sub list from vendor intelligence, drafts outreach | PM confirms before send |
| Bid leveling | Normalizes bid data, flags qualifications and scope gaps, calculates all-in cost | Estimator reviews; Buck awards |
| Buyout record | Creates structured award memo with scope terms | Buck signs |

| KPI | Target | Source |
|-----|--------|--------|

| Bid package issued without addenda | > 75% | Addendum log |

| Award within ROM budget | > 90% of scopes | Budget vs. award variance |
| Buyout complete before field mobilization | 100% | SOP 16 vs. NTP date |
