## THE RULE

**No AI system, workflow, automation, or agent may send a live email without explicit human approval.**

This is a standing directive with no exceptions.

---

## Required Email Workflow

Every email generated by the HCI AI OS must follow this exact sequence:

1. **Draft** — AI generates email content and saves to email draft folder only.
2. **Queue** — A corresponding item is created in the Approval Queue with: draft link, recipient, subject, project, reason for sending, and AI source.
3. **Review** — Buck (or delegated authority) reviews the draft in the email client.
4. **Approve** — Buck approves in the Approval Queue, setting `email_approved = true`.
5. **Send** — Only after approval flag is set may the email be released from drafts.

**If any step is skipped, the email must not send.**

---

## Technical Enforcement

Every code path, service, workflow, and n8n node that can send email must:

- Call only `create_draft()` — never `send_email()` directly.
- Check `approval_queue.email_approved` before any send operation.
- If `email_approved` is not `true`: save as draft, create approval queue item, stop.
- Log every draft creation with: timestamp, recipient, subject, project, source agent.

**Prohibited:** Any automation that calls a send endpoint without an approval gate.
